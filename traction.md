# Guava Admin Traction Snapshot (Data through 2025-11-03)

## User Growth Momentum
- Weekly new users now average **~20 per week**; even the most recent week (ending 3 Nov) added **10 net new customers** despite a post-launch normalization.
- Q3 was a breakout quarter with **318 new users** (+1,771% vs. Q2). Q4 has already onboarded **69** users just one month in.
- Monthly activations peaked at **110 users in September**, with a cumulative **298 signups since July** as we scaled the onboarding funnel.

## Engagement & Retention
- Monthly active users expanded from **2 (June)** to **15 (October)** before stabilizing at **4 active users so far in November**.
- Power usage is rising: October users averaged **12.4 transactions each** (up 59% vs. August), while September users peaked at **24.6 transactions per user**.
- Retention is improving: **100% of November’s active users are repeat customers**, compared to **40% in October** and **27% in September**.

## Revenue & Payments Scale
- Q4-to-date payment volume already reached **$4.96M** with **$120.9** in revenue, surpassing the entire Q3 revenue of **$16.0** by **7.5×**.
- October alone processed **$4.57M** across **186 transactions**, generating **$110.9** in fees (up from $15.1 in September).
- Annualized run-rate (Jan–Nov) sits at **$5.51M in volume** on **730 transactions**, yielding **$215** in fee revenue (avg. $0.29 per transaction).
- Weekly revenue spiked the week of 13 Oct to **$70.3**, demonstrating our ability to activate large corridors; recent weeks are stabilizing between **$7–$20** as we rebalance incentives.

## Product Usage Mix
- Bank payouts now account for **~99% of payment volume** (e.g., **$392K** bank transfers vs. **$65** wallet flows in November), showing strong adoption of our core treasury rails.
- Transaction mix remains diversified: wallet debits still drove **231 transactions** in September, giving us cross-sell opportunities for embedded services.
- Deposits are live (pilot launched June): **$3,000 in seed liquidity** processed with an average ticket of **$600**—we can scale this alongside payouts.

## Compliance & Verification Strength
- KYC throughput improved from a **23% approval rate in July** to **100% in November**, with total verifications steady between **10–22** per month.
- Active compliance coverage spans **three regions** since September, demonstrating multi-jurisdiction readiness.
- Current KYC funnel: **91.6% leads remain unverified**, **7.7% fully verified**, highlighting the upside from automating reminders and partner integrations.

## Geographic Footprint
- We now serve customers in **30+ countries**. Nigeria remains our largest base (**174 users, 41% share**) followed by the U.S. dollar corridor (**94 users, 22%**).
- Early traction across Europe, LATAM, MENA and Asia (e.g., **CHF, AED, ZAR, BRL, INR, SGD** exposures) validates our multi-currency infrastructure.
- Currency revenue mix reflects this global spread: NGN and USDC dominate today, but USD-null wallet flows contribute steady ancillary fees.

## Cohort & Lifetime Value Signals
- July and June cohorts are monetizing strongly with **$20–$26 LTV per user**, validating our treasury + FX bundle for early adopters.
- Newer cohorts (Aug–Oct) are still in activation mode; as they engage with bank transfers, LTV is expected to converge toward the July benchmark.
